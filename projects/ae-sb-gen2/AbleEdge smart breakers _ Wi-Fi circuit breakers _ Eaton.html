Circuit breakers

AbleEdge smart breakers

Pioneering technology for retrofit and new construction projects that transforms traditional loadcenters into modular smart panels. Our smart breakers enable intelligent load management, so you can power precisely what you need, when you need it:

  • Helping avoid main panel upgrades even as you add more energy loads
  • Delivering flexibility and scalability that reduces equipment costs and install time
  • Making it easier to add and manage solar and energy storage
  • Optimizing energy use and reducing costs 
  • Extending backup duration during grid outages  
Contact me about this solution

Eaton AbleEdge smart breakers transform existing and new Eaton BR loadcenters into modular smart panels.

Delivering flexibility and scalability that reduces equipment costs, installation time and simplifies electrification and decarbonization at home. Plus, industry collaboration with the biggest names in home solar and energy storage are accelerating a more sustainable, electrified future.

AbleEdge smart breakers are designed to integrate with a wide range of Eaton loadcenters and meter breakers.

Learn more

Open API platform

AbleEdge smart breakers support interoperability and Eaton is delivering deep integrations—making it easier for homeowners and electrical contractors to install and manage energy use with a single app.

AbleEdge Installer app

This application is designed for installers and contractors to seamlessly connect AbleEdge smart breakers to a Wi-Fi network and integrate it with a third-party organization, such as an Energy Storage System.
